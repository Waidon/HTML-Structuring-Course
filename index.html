My Journey as a Web Developer

My journey into web development began with a simple desire: to build something meaningful and shareable from scratch.

About me

My journey into web development started with a curiosity about how websites were built and the impact they could have on people and businesses.  At first, it felt overwhelming, there were so many languages, tools, and frameworks to explore.  But as I took my first steps learning HTML and CSS, I realized that building websites was not just about writing code, but about creating meaningful experiences for users.  This sparked a passion that continues to drive me today.

Over time, I began focusing on the foundations, making sure I understood not only how to structure pages, but how to do it the right way. I embraced semantic HTML to build clean, accessible, and professional websites.  From there, I expanded my knowledge into CSS for design and layout, JavaScript for interactivity, and even learning CSS frameworks for creating more dynamic applications.  Each project I built, whether a simple landing page, a personal portfolio, or a website for a business, it became a stepping stone to deepen my skills and apply what I was learning.

Alongside the technical skills, I’ve grown to appreciate the importance of design, user experience, and accessibility.  For me, web development is more than just code, it’s about solving problems, making information accessible, and giving individuals or businesses a digital presence they can be proud of.  My long-term goal is to keep growing as a front-end developer, while using what I learn to help others, whether through affordable web solutions, educational resources, or projects that bring communities together.
